Telegram Educational Tests Contest

With 2 billion students currently away from schools, the world is in dire need of online educational tools. Telegram would like to contribute to solving this problem by creating a database of educational tests for all subjects and levels. To do that, Telegram is announcing a crowd-sourcing initiative in which Telegram will distribute EUR 400,000 among creators of educational tests.
